Siberian cats are lovable and loyal family companions who get along well with children and other pets. Learn more about this rugged Russian cat breed. These cats are also known as a Russian national treasure. They have been documented in Russia for hundreds of years and are mentioned in Russian fairy tales and children’s books.

Characteristics

Coat Colour And Grooming

The Siberian is notable for having a long triple coat with guard hairs The undercoat thickens in cold weather. The coat comes in all colours and combinations of colours, with or without white.

Eyes

These cats are known for their round vibrant eyes. The outer corner angled slightly towards the base of the ear. Colours Can very however the most common is typically green or gold.

Temperment

They’re an active and playful breed and enjoy learning new tricks and games like fetch and that stimulate them mentally. This breed of cat loves to play with water and gets along well with other pets and children.

Personality

Besides being affectionate and loving, the Siberian is also alert and playful. Any item can become a plaything for this cat, so keep small keepsakes or other potentially intriguing items out of his sight. Their calm nature gives him the potential to be a therapy cat and I am speaking from experience! I have two Siberians at home and I am often taken back by how intune they are with my emotions. By far the two most snuggly cats I and visitors have ever met.
