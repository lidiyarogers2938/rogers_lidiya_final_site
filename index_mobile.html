Coat Colour And Grooming

The Siberian is notable for having a long triple coat with guard hairs The undercoat thickens in cold weather. The coat comes in all colours and combinations of colours, with or without white.

Personality

Besides being affectionate and loving, the Siberian is also alert and playful. Any item can become a plaything for this cat, so keep small keepsakes or other potentially intriguing items out of his sight. Their calm nature gives him the potential to be a therapy cat and I am speaking from experience! I have two Siberians at home and I am often taken back by how intune they are with my emotions. By far the two most snuggly cats I and visitors have ever met.
